About My Projects

This gallery showcases a diverse range of artistic projects created during my studies at Heartland Community College and through independent practice. Each piece represents my exploration of different mediums, techniques, and artistic styles.

From detailed sketching and realistic portraiture to abstract compositions and three-dimensional sculptures, these projects demonstrate my commitment to artistic growth and experimentation. I continue to develop my skills and explore new creative possibilities in every piece I create.
